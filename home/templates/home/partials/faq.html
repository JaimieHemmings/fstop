FAQs: Your Questions Answered

We understand that you may have questions about our services, pricing, and process. Feel free to reach out to us directly.

Photography is a totally bespoke service, so I don’t (or rather I can’t) offer fixed fee packages, as each shoot is as different as the images we capture. This is why we offer a free quotation service so that we can understand in detail what you’re looking for, which in turn allows me to provide the best price for you.

Yes, all of our photos are edited to ensure that they are of the highest quality. We use the latest editing software to enhance the colors, contrast, and sharpness of the images. We also remove any blemishes or imperfections to ensure that you look your best in every photo. This service is included in your quote, so you don't have to worry about any additional costs.

Per UK law the copyright to the photos belongs to the photographer, but you will have full usage rights to the images. This means that you can use the photos for personal or commercial purposes without any restrictions. You can print the photos, share them on social media, or use them in your marketing materials. If you have any specific requirements for the usage of the photos, please let us know and we will be happy to accommodate your needs.

We will provide you with the photos in a digital format, which you can download from our website. The photos will be high-resolution and suitable for printing, as well as low-resolution versions that are perfect for sharing online. If you have any specific requirements for the format of the photos, please let us know and we will be happy to accommodate your needs.

Yes, we have full public liability insurance, which covers us for any accidents or incidents that may occur during the shoot. We also have professional indemnity insurance, which covers us for any errors or omissions in our work. This means that you can have peace of mind knowing that you are fully protected when you work with us.

Typically, from the initial enquiry we can deliver your photos within 5 to 6 weeks but the timeframe for your shoot will depend on the complexity of the project and the number of photos required. We will work with you to establish a timeline that meets your needs and ensures that you receive your photos in a timely manner. We will keep you informed of our progress throughout the process and will provide you with regular updates on the status of your photos. If you have any specific deadlines that you need to meet, please let us know and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.
