Based in rural Cornwall F. works with clients throughout the South West and the rest of the UK. With photography experience stretching back over 15 years be rest assured that we bring the expertise needed to bring your vision to life.

We specialize in “On-Location” and “Usage” projects. By that we mean capturing products and services that cannot be brought into a studio situation (though we do that as well) and the photographing of specific events. Examples include photographing businesses themselves where the project must of course be photographed at the specific location OR lifestyle type shots where a product is immersed into the environment where it will be used.

We also take product imagery in a studio here in Cornwall so are able to accept projects remotely and provide a full service to clients including models.

Our passion for “On-Location” photography extends to personal portfolio imagery, images being captured for Instagram and other social media platforms as well as weddings and party events.
